ABOUT ME

Thanks for stopping by! I'm Jason, a passionate web designer and developer with a first-class honours degree in Computer Science, looking to use my technical skills to make a positive impact on the world.

While studying in Edinburgh, I worked part-time as a web developer for a small web agency in Aberdeen. During this time, I participated in an innovation competition and won the highest award for my start-up, readysetrota, which sparked my interest in public speaking and entrepreneurship. After two years as a web developer, I became a software instructor at CodeClan, where I refined my communication skills while deepening my programming knowledge.

In 2023, CodeClan went into liquidation and my role as an instructor came to an end. I embraced the opportunity and embarked on a year-long adventure in Australia. I worked as a Junior Sommelier in Melbourne and am currently bartending on the Sunshine Coast. If you’re looking for someone to make barista-level Australian coffees in the office, look no further!

Now, I'm excited to return to the tech industry, seeking my next opportunity in the UK or Australia where I can contribute my technical expertise, creativity, and interpersonal skills. Let’s connect if you're looking for someone to bring fresh ideas to your team!
